Fundamentals of
Database Systems

This course introduces the principles and practical skills needed to design, create, query, secure, and maintain reliable database systems. Students study relational models, SQL, entity-relationship design, normalization, transactions, recovery, NoSQL, and cloud databases while building hands-on experience with MySQL and phpMyAdmin.
